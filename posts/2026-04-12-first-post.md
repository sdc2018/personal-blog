I've been building companies, raising kids, and thinking about life for two decades. Most of that thinking stayed in my head or in private notes.

It's time to write in public.

This blog is where I'll share thoughts on building a business, the pursuit of a good life, and the things I'm learning along the way. No polish, no corporate speak — just honest reflections from a founder in Mumbai who's trying to figure things out.

## What to expect

- Lessons from running a 37-person consulting firm
- Thoughts on data, AI, and what's actually useful vs. hype
- Observations on life, patience, and the long game

Let's see where this goes.
